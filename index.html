ABOUT ME

UX Designer

I am a User Experience Designer. What does this mean to me? UX Design is about designing with empathy for the user. UX Design is about designing a specific experience that is empathetically-driven. It takes into account not only the aesthetic or function, but the emotions of delight and ease-of-use. It is not about the process, but the journey of emotions a user feels along the way.
