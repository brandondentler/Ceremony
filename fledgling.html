Fledgling Games/CRLT


January 2020 - January 2021

Overview

Fledgling Games is a studio-based internship at Purdue for students to gain real-world experience within the video game industry. I worked on a project for Purdue Labs in times of COVID. The project we worked on was making it easier for students to complete their labs from the comfort of their own home/dorm.

For this experience, I worked as the Lead UX Designer, a team member, as well as an overall consultant for design decisions.

I also worked on a project for Crisis Response Leadership Training (CRLT) . CRLT is a startup video game company striving to prepare and certify emergency responders for extreme scenarios through a simulation.

For this experience, I was an intern working as the Lead UX designer to create user stories ranging from personas, user journeys, scenarios and many other forms. I was also a co-lead for the UI design in helping create the experience and flow of the video game.

The Solution

Bring VR technology into TV to allow their users to feel like they are attending a sporting event and can have a social experience from the comfort of their own home. I want to design a VR prototype that combines the VR experience and a live sporting event where the viewer is “sitting in the stands” surrounded by other VR members, family, friends, or strangers.

CRLT

At CRLT, I was a member at the start of the project. This meant I was manily tasked with doing research on:

  • Personalization
  • Other strategy games and top-down perspectives
  • Tutorial Styles
  • UI Radial Menus
  • Inventory Systems
  • And a Competitive Analysis

Other deliverables I completed were Journey Maps, Personas, Player Personas, a Reddit Review, Stakeholder Map, and User scenarios
